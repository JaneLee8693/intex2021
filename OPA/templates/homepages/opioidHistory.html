History of the Opioid Crisis
  • Overdose Waves
    • 1st Wave: Dramatic increase in prescribed overdoses in 1990s
    • 2nd Wave: Illegal opioid Heroin sees a dramatic increase in overdose in 2010
    • 3rd Wave: Synthetic Opioids including Fentanyl sees a dramatic increase in overdose in 2013
  • 2016
    • The CDC collects numbers regarding: people with a use disorder and the opioid type, number of overdoses and deaths.
  • 2017 Trump declared the opioid crisis a public health emergency after the yearly death toll from opioids reaches 70,237
